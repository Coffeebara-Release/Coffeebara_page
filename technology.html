Revolutionizing Coffee Cup Recycling

Our innovative technology transforms used coffee cups into valuable resources, contributing to a sustainable future.

Sorting Mechanism

Our advanced sorting system uses optical sensors and AI to identify and separate different types of coffee cups, ensuring only recyclable materials are processed.

Cleaning Process

A multi-stage cleaning process removes any remaining liquids or contaminants from the cups, preparing them for material processing.

Material Processing

The cleaned cups are shredded and processed into high-quality recycled materials, suitable for various applications, including new products.

Innovative Technologies

Our patented technologies ensure efficient and effective recycling, minimizing waste and maximizing resource recovery. We are committed to continuous innovation to improve our processes.
