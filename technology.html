Application

The 'NOCO' Process

How we apply our core tech to solve the disposable cup crisis.

1

Recognition

Users simply "Drop" the cup. Our Edge AI analyzes the object in real-time, identifying material type, size, and presence of lids or straws.

  • 99.9% Detection Accuracy
  • Multi-object Classification
2

Separation

The robotic arm, equipped with our adaptive gripper, deftly separates lids and holders from the cup body. Parallel processing ensures high throughput.

  • Non-destructive handling
  • Handles 5+ cup sizes
3

Cleansing

High-pressure fluid jets remove residue and contaminants (sugar, milk) in seconds, significantly increasing the value of the recycled PET material.

  • Minimal Water Usage
  • High-speed Drying
4

Loading

Cleaned cups are stacked vertically to maximize storage efficiency. Our smart stacking algorithm reduces volume by up to 90% compared to traditional bins.

  • 10x Volume Reduction
  • IoT Full-Level Alerts
