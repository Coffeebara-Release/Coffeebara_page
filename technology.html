Recognition By Image Analyzer

Our system utilizes a YOLO-based AI model to automatically determine the size of a cup and whether a holder is present. By applying state-of-the-art visual object detection techniques, the model accurately analyzes real-time images to identify and classify cup attributes. This enables fully automated and reliable inspection for downstream processes in manufacturing or quality control environments.

Separation Process

In this stage, our system automatically separates the lid from the cup and classifies both the holder and straw. All of these processes operate in parallel, allowing the handling and sorting of multiple cups simultaneously. Using precise robotic mechanisms, the system ensures efficient and accurate separation and classification, enabling high-throughput and reliable operation for real-world production environments.

Cleansing

In this stage, high-pressure water jets are used to thoroughly wash the inside of each cup, ensuring that no residue remains. This advanced cleaning process enables the hygienic and efficient recycling of single-use plastic cups, helping to promote sustainability and facilitate the reuse of plastic materials.

Loading Process

At this stage, cups are automatically sorted and stacked according to their size. This size-based loading process maximizes the system’s capacity and efficiency, allowing for optimal storage and organization of cleaned cups. By neatly grouping cups by size, the system greatly improves overall throughput and readiness for subsequent recycling or reuse processes.
